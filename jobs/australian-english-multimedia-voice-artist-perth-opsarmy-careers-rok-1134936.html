Job description

Australian English Multimedia Voice Artist

Apply here: Application Form Link

We are seeking talented Australian English Multimedia Voice Artists to support AI, localisation, and digital media projects through the creation of high-quality spoken content. The ideal candidate can deliver clear, natural, and engaging recordings while adapting to a variety of content styles and audiences.

Responsibilities

  • Record professional-quality audio in Australian English using provided scripts and guidelines.
  • Deliver expressive and natural voice performances appropriate to the content.
  • Adapt tone, pacing, and speaking style to meet project requirements.
  • Follow pronunciation standards and recording instructions.
  • Review recordings to ensure accuracy, consistency, and audio quality.
  • Submit completed recordings within established deadlines.
  • Communicate any content, pronunciation, or technical concerns to the project team.
  • Maintain professionalism and consistency throughout all project assignments.


Requirements

  • Native or near-native proficiency in Australian English.
  • Authentic Australian accent and pronunciation.
  • Excellent pronunciation, articulation, and verbal communication skills.
  • Strong reading comprehension and attention to detail.
  • Ability to adapt delivery for different audiences and content types.
  • Reliable internet connection and access to a quiet recording environment.
  • Basic computer skills and familiarity with online recording platforms.
  • Ability to work independently and manage deadlines effectively.


Nice to Have

  • Previous experience in voice-over, narration, dubbing, podcasting, broadcasting, or media production.
  • Experience with AI, speech data collection, or localisation projects.
  • Familiarity with audio recording and editing software.
  • Access to professional recording equipment and a sound-treated recording space.
