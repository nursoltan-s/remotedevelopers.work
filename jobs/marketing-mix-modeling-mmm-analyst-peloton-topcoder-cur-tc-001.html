Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) Analyst — Peloton engagement via Topcoder.

We are seeking a Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) Analyst to develop and refine statistical models that measure marketing channel effectiveness and optimize budget allocation. This role combines advanced statistical expertise with business acumen to translate complex analytical outputs into actionable insights for executive leadership.

Responsibilities

  • Experience range: 4 to 15 years in total to handle the project
  • Extract, clean, and validate large, complex marketing and external datasets using SQL and Python/R
  • Develop and refine Bayesian MMM and econometric models (lag effects, seasonality, multicollinearity)
  • Analyze MMM outputs for ROI by channel, saturation curves, and budget allocation scenarios
  • Create executive-ready presentations and visualizations
  • Automate workflows with SQL, Python, R, and visualization platforms
  • Deploy and maintain models using tools such as Google Meridian

Requirements

  • Proficiency in SQL
  • Strong Python or R for statistical modeling
  • Expertise in Bayesian MMM and econometric modeling
  • Experience with complex datasets and multicollinearity
  • Ability to communicate technical findings to non-technical stakeholders
  • Experience with data visualization platforms

Nice to have

  • Google Meridian or similar MMM platforms
  • Time-series analysis
  • Marketing analytics / BI background
  • Version control and collaborative development

Duration: 52 weeks · Location: Remote · Workload: Full Time · Compensation: Negotiable
