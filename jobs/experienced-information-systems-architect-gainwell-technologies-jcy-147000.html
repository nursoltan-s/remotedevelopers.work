Summary

The Advisor Information Systems Architect – ServiceNow is a senior-level technical leader responsible for designing, implementing, and governing enterprise-wide ServiceNow solutions. This role owns the architectural integrity of the platform, with a strong focus on integrations, CMDB alignment, and platform scalability. The Advisor collaborates closely with the Platform Architect, CMDB Admin, and cross-functional teams to ensure that ServiceNow capabilities align with business goals and ITSM best practices.

Your role in our mission

  • Lead the design and evolution of the ServiceNow platform architecture, ensuring alignment with enterprise standards and business needs
  • Define and enforce platform governance, including release management and security protocols
  • Provide architectural solutions for integrating third-party tools into the corporate ServiceNow instance
  • Design, document, and maintain the information architecture for the ServiceNow platform, ensuring it aligns with enterprise architecture and data governance standards.
  • Lead the modeling and structuring of data within ServiceNow applications (e.g., ITSM, ITOM, CMDB, SAM, HAM, SecOps, VR, GRC, CSM).
  • Partner with platform engineers, product owners, developers, and process owners to define data flows, integrations, and taxonomies.
  • Evaluate and enforce naming conventions, data standards, and reference models across the ServiceNow environment.
  • Guide the optimization of CMDB health, data integrity, and relationship modeling (CI class structures)
