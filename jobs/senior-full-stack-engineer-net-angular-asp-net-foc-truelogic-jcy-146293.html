Job Summary

We are seeking a Senior Full-Stack Engineer to support and modernize a mission-critical .NET platform with a strong focus on security, performance, and clean architecture. This role spans backend C#, ASP.NET Core, and SQL Server development, alongside front-end technologies including jQuery, Kendo UI, Bootstrap, and incremental Angular migration. The engineer will lead structured modernization initiatives, vulnerability remediation, dependency upgrades, and CI/CD improvements while ensuring secure coding practices, scalability, and long-term platform stability within an agile environment.
