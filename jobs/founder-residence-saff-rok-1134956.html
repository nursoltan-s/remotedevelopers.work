About Saff

Saff is a six-week residency for Muslim founders who have already shipped. You move into one house in London with nine other founders, build a go-to-market system around your company, and close with a demo day where half the room are buyers.


What you walk out with

  • A mapped list of your hundred target accounts and thirty dream buyers, built in week one
  • A live pipeline: buyer meetings engineered during the season, not after it
  • Residency fully covered: travel, stay, food, production
  • Mentors in residence, plus visiting operators and angels, weekly angel dinners

Who we're looking for

  • Already building: you've shipped a product with real users or early revenue, no ideas on paper
  • Able to commit: you can live and build in residence in London for the full six weeks
  • Muslim founders building across the UK, Europe, or North America, solo or co-founded
  • You know your buyer: you can name the five customers that would change your company, and we'll help you reach them
