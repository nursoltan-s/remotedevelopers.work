Job description

Kettle is a team of passionate, fearless creatives focused on delivering great products, redefining best-practices, and providing new opportunities for our clients.

We are looking for a Senior Product Designer with a focus on Design Systems to join our Product team. In this role, you’ll work on one of our largest accounts, contributing to a design system that touches millions of people per year.

Candidates should have:

  • Interaction or product design experience, designing iOS and Android native applications and websites
  • Experience creating or maintaining enterprise-level design systems
  • Maintained Figma libraries across both native and web platforms
  • Experience in creating documentation for single components and larger UX patterns
  • Worked with engineering teams for hand-offs and visual QA
  • Experience helping product organizations adopt new design systems
  • A clear communication style, with the ability to present design work internally for feedback

We’d like to know that you can:

  • Design creative product experiences from initial concepting through detailed design and collaboration with development
  • Solve complex experience challenges while delivering beautiful, usable interfaces
  • Bring a high-level of creativity and creative problem solving to each project, and be comfortable with pushing boundaries
  • Create both designs and detailed annotations that are informative and understandable for clients, designers and devs
  • Create prototypes to explain key interactions and flows
  • Work independently on projects with the guidance of your director
  • Partner with teammates of different disciplines including Product Management, Production, and Development
  • At the Lead level, manage multiple projects within a larger pod, and mentor other designers
  • Adapt to different working styles and processes, from quick turn optimization to blue sky visioning
  • Embrace challenges, not afraid to get your hands dirty and solution with fellow teammates and clients

The target range of compensation is $110k - $135k. Actual compensation is influenced by a wide array of factors including but not limited to skill set, level of experience, and location.
