We’re looking for creative thinkers with hands-on experience in Agentforce, AI agent development, and intelligent process automation, who thrive in a collaborative, fast-paced environment. As a Senior Application Engineer specializing in Salesforce Agentforce AI at PointClickCare, you’ll architect and deliver state-of-the-art AI agents and automations that transform system workflows. You’ll be empowered to experiment, prototype, and deliver solutions that directly shape the future of our technology landscape, gain exposure to the latest AI technologies and dedicated time for professional development and certifications as you create AI Agents using Agentforce and other AI tools.
 
To succeed in this role, you need to be an autonomous and accountable team player with a continuous learning mindset and understand what it means to be a member of a high performing team. You bring hands-on experience building Agentforce AI Agents and over 5 years of experience and knowledge designing, developing, and maintaining configuration and custom applications that integrate with Salesforce or other connected apps. 
 
As a Senior Application Engineer (Salesforce Agentforce AI) at PointClickCare you will also build intelligent process automations, test automations, and debug standard to complex issues related with Apex code, JavaScript, Python or code from other programming languages as it relates to the applications you are developing for. You will develop technical documentation, be part of our DevOps maintenance crew and peer review builds developed by other team members and vendors. You will provide training and consultation related to the systems and processes developed by the team to our business partners and support both transformational projects, high priority enhancements and resolve standard to high complexity incidents to quickly deliver value to the business.
 
Key duties and responsibilities:
 
•Work at the forefront of AI-driven Salesforce solutions and build AI Agents using Agentforce, and other AI platforms like Microsoft Studio for CoPilot Agents.
•Pioneer intelligent automations in enterprise environments using IPA tools like Power Automate, Alteryx, and others.
•Build test automations to improve QA efficiencies in QA for Salesforce and other applications.
•Collaborate with product designers, end-users, and other team members to refine and understand outcomes and AI use cases.
•Collaborate with other team members to brainstorm ideas for prototyping to test and solution for use cases identified and prioritized.
•Execute standard to complex tasks in the application development life cycle using languages like Apex, JavaScript, Python, etc., etc., relevant to the core applications (Salesforce, Billing Platform, etc.) and connected or integrated apps (Conga, Docusign, Adobe, Marketo, Gainsight, Gong, Qualtrics, Kantata, TaskRay, etc.). 
•Perform unit tests for your builds and collaborate with Product Designers and other team members to create accurate and quality test scripts for automated and manual testing.
•Analyze, troubleshoot, and debug systems for standard issues
•Be an AI change agent in your team to gain team buy-in for adopting use of AI agents to save time, reduce waste and improve efficiency to deliver value to customers faster
•Create and maintain technical documentation for all builds and AI Agents produced
•Onboard, mentor and coach Junior - Intermediate application engineers and your peers – including peer reviewing their work, providing feedback, approving pull requests and providing guidance as needed. 
•Perform other tasks and duties as assigned
 
Skills and Qualifications: 
•Hands-on experience building Agentforce AI Agents, AI workflows, custom agent development, and integration of external data sources. •Solid experience with Salesforce integrations and external system integrations (Conga, Docusign, Adobe, Marketo, Gainsight, Gong, Qualtrics, Kantata, TaskRay.• Working experience with Workday platform highly desired
