About Us

remotedevelopers is a curated job board for remote engineers — nothing else.

Why we exist

Most job boards bury remote roles under hybrid noise, recruiter spam, and vague “work from anywhere” posts that still expect you in an office three days a week. We built remotedevelopers for developers who want verified remote work — with transparent pay when possible, clear timezone expectations, and listings worth your time.

What we curate

  • Remote-only engineering roles — no hybrid or on-site filler
  • Salary bands when employers share them
  • Paid posts reviewed by hand before they go live
  • Partner listings from boards like Himalayas, Remote OK, Jobicy, and Arbeitnow — clearly marked as syndicated

For employers

Post a job for a flat $5 one-time fee. No monthly plans, no seat limits. Listings are reviewed (usually within 24 hours) so the feed stays useful for candidates.

Get in touch

Questions about a listing, partnership, or the site? Email hello@remotedevelopers.work or visit our contact page.
