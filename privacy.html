Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

remotedevelopers is a static website. We do not operate user accounts. Theme preference may be stored in your browser’s localStorage.

When you submit a job posting, the information you enter is used only to review and publish the listing. Payment is processed by third-party providers (e.g. Stripe or PayPal) when connected; we do not store card numbers on this site.

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand site traffic. It is privacy-oriented and does not use cookies for tracking. It collects anonymized visit metrics such as page views and referrers. Hosting providers may also collect standard server logs. External “Apply” links send you to third-party sites governed by their own policies.

For privacy questions, contact us via the domain email when published.
