Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

1. Who we are

remotedevelopers (“we”, “us”) operates https://remotedevelopers.work, a curated remote job board for software developers. Contact us at hello@remotedevelopers.work.

2. Information we collect

  • Job posting details you submit (company name, role, salary, description, contact email) so we can review and publish listings.
  • Browser storage for theme preference (light/dark) via localStorage. This stays on your device.
  • Usage metrics from privacy-oriented analytics and standard hosting logs (see below).
  • Advertising data when Google AdSense or similar partners serve ads (see Advertising).

We do not require user accounts to browse jobs. We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data.

3. How we use information

  • Review, publish, and manage job listings
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Understand traffic and improve the site
  • Show relevant advertising and measure ad performance
  • Prevent spam, abuse, and fraud

4. Analytics

We may use Cloudflare Web Analytics or similar privacy-oriented tools to understand anonymized visit metrics (page views, referrers, device type). These tools are designed to minimize personal data and typically do not use cookies for cross-site tracking. Our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may also collect standard server and security logs.

5. Advertising

We use Google AdSense and related Google advertising services to display ads. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites. This may include personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your region and consent settings.

You can learn how Google uses data when you use partners’ sites or apps at Google’s Partner Sites & Apps policy, and manage ad personalization at Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of interest-based ads from participating companies via aboutads.info.

6. Payments

Job posting payments are processed by third-party providers such as Gumroad. We do not store card numbers on this site. Payment processors handle billing data under their own privacy policies.

7. Third-party links

Job “Apply” links and partner listings send you to external company or job-board sites. Those sites are governed by their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

8. Data retention & security

Job submission emails and related correspondence are kept as long as needed to operate the board and comply with legal obligations. We use reasonable technical measures (HTTPS, reputable hosting) to protect information, but no method of transmission is 100% secure.

9. Your choices

  • Clear localStorage in your browser to remove theme preference
  • Use browser cookie controls and Google’s ad settings
  • Email us to request deletion of a job submission or contact record we hold

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. Contact us to exercise those rights.

10. Children’s privacy

This site is intended for adults seeking or posting professional employment. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the equivalent age in your jurisdiction).

11. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

12. Contact

Privacy questions: hello@remotedevelopers.work
